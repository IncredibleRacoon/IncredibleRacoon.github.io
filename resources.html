Resources

A curated list of essential links for hardware engineers.

Distributors & Parts

  • DigiKey Massive inventory, detailed parametric search.
  • Mouser Great for new product introduction (NPI).
  • LCSC Affordable Asian parts, pairs well with JLCPCB.
  • Octopart Search engine for electronic components.

PCB Manufacturing

  • JLCPCB Cheap, fast, good for prototypes.
  • PCBWay More advanced options (flex, rigid-flex).
  • Aisler European based (Germany), high quality.
  • OSH Park Purple PCBs, high quality US manufacturing.

Learning & Reference

  • EEVBlog The gold standard for electronics engineering entertainment.
  • Altium Academy High quality deep-dives into PCB design topics.
  • All About Circuits Articles, textbooks, and forums.
  • Pinout.xyz The best Raspberry Pi GPIO pinout guide.

Advanced Tools

  • Ultra Librarian Huge database of CAD models (symbols, footprints, 3D).
  • Saturn PCB Toolkit The industry standard calculator (Windows).
  • DC/DC Converter Design Calculators for Buck/Boost topologies.
